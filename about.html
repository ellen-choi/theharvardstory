About



Welcome to the Harvard Story! This is a website for all of those who have fallen in love with the Crimson community. When someone utters the word Harvard, the common thought that passes through many heads is "the prestigious university". Although true, the school has much more history and color than this nickname. Therefore, this website is dedicated to relaying all the hidden stories of Harvard - from beautiful buildings to eccentric events.

But of course, we can't leave out a brief description of what kind of univerity Harvard is:

  • Known as a private Ivy League research university in Cambridge, Massachusetts
  • Established in 1636 as the oldest institution of higher education in the United States
  • Has an enrollment of over 20,000 degree candidates and more than 360,000 alumni around the world
  • Ranked #1 university in the nation and world according to several sites
